## App Review notes (paste into App Store Connect)

### What the app does
freestylefit lets users log clothing try-ons (size + fit rating + optional notes) by pasting a product URL and saving a fit entry to their account.

### How to test core flow
1. Create an account (Sign Up) or sign in.
2. Go to **Scan** tab.
3. Paste a product URL and tap **Look Up**.
4. Tap **Select Size & Rate Fit**.
5. Complete the steps and tap **Save Try-On**.
6. Verify the saved entry appears in the **Closet** tab.
