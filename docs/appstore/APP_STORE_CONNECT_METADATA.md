Stop guessing your size. freestylefit helps you track what fits across brands.

Log a try-on in seconds:
- Paste a product link to look it up
- Choose the size you tried
- Rate the fit (too small / perfect / too large)
- Add optional notes and details

Build your personal fit history so you can:
- Remember what worked (and what didn’t)
- Compare fit across brands
- Shop faster with less uncertainty

freestylefit is built for people who want fewer returns and more confidence when shopping.
