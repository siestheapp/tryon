Support — freestylefit

If you need help, contact: support@freestylefit.net

Common issues

  • Can’t sign in: double-check your email/password and your network connection.
  • Product link not found: verify the URL is from a supported brand and is the full product page link.
  • App feels stuck: force-close and reopen the app, then try again.

Feature requests

Send ideas to the support email above with:

  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • Screenshots (optional)
